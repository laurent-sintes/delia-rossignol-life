ENTER THE GAME 🎮

As Games Delivery Expert, you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth delivery of casino games to market. You will coordinate monthly game releases, manage game configurations in our back-office systems, and work closely with QA, compliance, product, and customer support to guarantee high-quality player experiences. You will become the go-to expert on casino games—how they work, how they are configured, and how to optimize their performance.

This is a hands-on operational role that requires strong ownership, precision, collaboration, and a passion for gaming technology.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

  • Game Release Management
    • Own the monthly game release schedule across multiple markets and suppliers
    • Coordinate with product managers, suppliers, and internal stakeholders to ensure on-time delivery
    • Manage game approvals and certifications ahead of release
  • Game Configuration
    • Configure games accurately in our back-office systems (parameters, promotions, lobbies, RTP versions, tags, etc.)
    • Ensure correct integration settings between platform and game providers
    • Maintain consistent taxonomy and metadata for optimal user experience
  • Quality Assurance
    • Perform pre-release QA and functional testing on all game launches
    • Identify issues and collaborate with suppliers to resolve them
    • Monitor live games performance and escalate incidents when required
  • Compliance
    • Ensure games comply with regulatory requirements in all jurisdictions where we operate
    • Validate certifications, RNG documentation, and responsible gaming standards
    • Maintain up-to-date compliance records
  • Support & Game Knowledge
    • Act as the internal subject-matter expert for casino games
    • Provide 2nd-line support for player related issues raised by Customer Support
    • Create and maintain internal documentation and release notes
