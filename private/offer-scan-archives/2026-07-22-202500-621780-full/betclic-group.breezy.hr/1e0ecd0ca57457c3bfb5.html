ENTER THE GAME 🎮

As a member of the Defensive team, you will join the Global Security teams to:

  1. Operate cybersecurity threat detection and incident response activities
  2. Implement threat engineering, detection mechanisms, and incident response procedures
  3. Participate in innovative projects to redesign AI-driven SOC and CSIRT operations

    YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

    In this role, your main missions will be:

    • Monitor security events and alerts accross systems, networks, and applications
    • Perform incident response and containment actions
    • Develop and maintain detection rules, use cases and alerting mechanisms
    • Conduct threat analysis, threat hunting and threat intelligence to identify attacks
    • Reduce false positives and optimize detection efficiency
    • Automate repetitive tasks end enhance operational efficiency with AI
    • Contribute to cybersecurity projects and/or programs

    WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR? 🔍

    We are looking for passionate and curious professionals who bring kindness and a touch of enthusiasm!

    This apprenticeship is for you if several of the following apply:

    • You have a strong academic background and a solid understanding of systems, networks, cryptography, software development, web and cloud environments.
    • You have gained some academic or hands-on experience with defensive or intelligence activities (SOC, CSIRT, OSINT, CTI...) and security tools (SIEM, EDR, XDR, honeypots, NDR...).
    • You have a basic understanding of forensic tools and investigation techniques.
    • You have explored offensive security techniques and participated in CTF competitions, Bug Bounty programs, or learning platforms (Root-Me, HTB...).
    • You are eager to learn about modern IT architectures, including cloud, identity, containers, and related technologies.
    • You are enthusiastic about building AI-driven tools for next-generation SOC.
    • Finally, you are comfortable working in English and motivated to continuously develop your skills.

    WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS? 📝

    If your application is shortlisted, Maxime will contact you within a week for an initial HR screening (30 minutes). Then, you will complete the AssessFirst test (personality, motivation and cognitive reasoning).

    Next, you will meet the Defensive cybersecurity manager. Finally, Maxime will conduct a final HR interview to review your AssessFirst results together.

    The recruitment process usually takes between 4 to 6 weeks to ensure an optimal experience for you.
