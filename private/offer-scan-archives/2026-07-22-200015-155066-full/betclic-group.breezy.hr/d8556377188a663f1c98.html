ENTER THE GAME 🎮

To support Betclic’s growth, we are looking for a new analyst, to join our Operational Excellence team in the Safer Gaming department.

Since its creation, the Safer Gaming department has aimed to provide a protective framework for players to safeguard minors and players who may have a problem with gambling.

The Operational Excellence team’s mission is to bring customer protection to the next level, by identifying and working on ways to improve the way we work; either through continuous improvement and small iterations, or through radical, innovative changes!

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC🔥

As a Safer Gaming Quality Analyst, your missions are the following:

Quality analysis:
o Perform quality assessments of our daily operations
o Liaise with the operations teams to correct issues spotted during quality checks
o Report quality assessment outputs, illustrating key performance metrics, to management

Knowledge:
o Master all aspects of operational processes and workflows
o Benchmark best market practice in terms of customer protection
o Back up the Safer gaming training team with targeted coaching and training

Quality system definition:
o Contribute to operational performance improvement through targeted recommendations, in collaboration with the training team
o Contribute to continuous improvement of our quality system
