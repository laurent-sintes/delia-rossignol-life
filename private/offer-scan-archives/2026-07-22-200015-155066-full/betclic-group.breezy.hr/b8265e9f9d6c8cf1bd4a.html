WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS? 📝

If your application is selected, Maxime will contact you within a week for an initial HR screening call (30 minutes).
You will then be invited to complete the AssessFirst test (covering personality, motivations, and cognitive abilities).

Next, you’ll meet with your future manager for a technical and team-fit interview.
Finally, you’ll have a final HR interview with Maxime, where you’ll also review and discuss your AssessFirst results together.

To ensure a smooth and candidate-friendly experience, the Betclic recruitment process typically takes between 4 to 6 weeks.
