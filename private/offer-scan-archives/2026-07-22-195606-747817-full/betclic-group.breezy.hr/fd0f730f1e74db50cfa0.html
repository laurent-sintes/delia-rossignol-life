ENTER THE GAME 🎮

Following the combination of Betclic, Tipico, and Admiral, we are building a best-in-class Group Finance function to support the future of Banijay Gaming, a €3bn revenue business operating across multiple jurisdictions. This is a unique opportunity to join the Finance Strategic team and play a central role in one of the Group's most ambitious transformation programmes.

As Finance Transformation Project Manager, you will initially lead the ERP strategy, Finance systems assessment, and implementation programme before progressively coordinating a broader portfolio of Finance Transformation initiatives. Working closely with Finance, Technology, Procurement, Data, Operations, and executive leadership, you will help define the future Finance technology landscape and deliver the Group Finance Target Operating Model.


YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

In this role, your main missions will be:

ERP & P2P strategy & selection program

  • Manage the Group ERP & P2P strategy and selection initiative
  • Assess the current ERP, Finance, and Procure-to-Pay landscape across Betclic, Tipico, and Admiral
  • Ensure ERP decisions support the future Group Finance operating model and combination objectives
  • Coordinate all advisory activities, workshops, deliverables, and stakeholder engagement
  • Review and challenge advisory recommendations to ensure alignment with Group objectives
  • Monitor timelines, risks, dependencies, budget, and quality of deliverables
  • Drive alignment and ensure effective collaboration between advisors, Finance teams, IT teams, and executive stakeholders
  • Prepare decision materials and recommendations for executive leadership and steering/operational committees

Finance Transformation Programme Management

  • Manage the ERP and P2P implementation program across the Group
  • Act as primary counterpart to the ERP editor and implementation partner
  • Coordinate multiple workstreams across Finance, Procurement, Technology, Data, and Operations
  • Coordinate business process and design workshops,
  • Ensure alignment between system design and future-state operating model
  • Facilitate decision-making across Finance, Procurement, IT, and Transformation stakeholders
  • Minimize unnecessary customizations and promote fit-to-standard approaches
  • Lead the data migration strategy and execution
  • Drive the Change Management & Business Readiness
