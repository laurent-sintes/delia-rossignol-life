ENTER THE GAME 🎮

At Betclic, we are on a mission to redefine the online sports betting and gaming experience. At the heart of this ambition is our Customer Experience team a dynamic group of product designers, UX researchers, and product leaders dedicated to shaping the future of the Betclic product experience.

As our Design Systems & AI Design Ops, you will help shape the next generation of our design system. Beyond maintaining consistency across products, your mission will be to make the system increasingly scalable, AI-ready, and deeply connected to engineering workflows.

You will sit at the intersection of Design, Engineering and AI, helping define how products are designed, documented and built at scale.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

In this role, your main missions will be:

Design System Strategy, Scale & AI Readiness

  • Own and evolve the design system as a product, ensuring consistency, scalability and alignment with engineering teams
  • Ensure seamless integration between design and front-end development through close collaboration with engineers
  • Drive adoption of the design system across teams and ensure its effective and consistent usage
  • Define governance models, contribution processes, validation workflows and versioning strategies
  • Structure a scalable, maintainable and interoperable design system architecture
  • Lead the evolution of design tokens and foundational system elements
  • Define reusable interaction patterns that go beyond component libraries
  • Build machine-readable systems and documentation consumable by both humans and AI agents
  • Ensure the design system is AI-ready, AI-consumable and tightly connected to engineering workflows
  • Create and maintain high-quality documentation supporting both teams and AI-powered processes

AI-Powered Design Systems & Workflows

  • Define how AI transforms design production and design system management
  • Explore and implement ways for AI to leverage the design system and accelerate product creation
  • Evaluate, test and scale emerging AI tools such as Claude Code, Cursor, Figma AI, Figma Make and other innovative solutions
  • Build AI-native workflows connecting Figma, documentation platforms, code repositories and design tokens
  • Explore MCPs, AI agents and structured knowledge systems
  • Structure documentation and knowledge bases to maximize AI usability and efficiency
  • Establish best practices, governance frameworks and guardrails for AI adoption
  • Enable teams to confidently embrace AI-powered workflows and new ways of working

Design Operations & Workflow Excellence

  • Continuously improve workflows across the entire design lifecycle
  • Define best practices for Figma organization, scalability and collaboration
  • Set up design reviews and quality gates to ensure system excellence
  • Identify inefficiencies and implement improvements that increase team velocity
  • Create lightweight and scalable operational frameworks where necessary

Adoption, Culture & Impact

  • Drive design system adoption across Product and Engineering organizations
  • Promote systems thinking, innovation and experimentation
  • Act as a bridge between Design, Product and Engineering teams
  • Support teams in understanding and leveraging AI-enabled design workflows
  • Increase overall design leverage, efficiency and organizational impact

TECHNICAL ENVIRONMENT

  • Figma, Figma AI, Figma Make
  • Design Systems & Design Tokens
  • Front-End Design-to-Code Workflows
  • Storybook, Chromatic, Code Connect
  • Claude Code, Cursor and AI-native tooling
  • MCPs (Model Context Protocols) and AI Agents
  • Documentation Platforms & Structured Knowledge Bases
  • Versioning, Governance & Design Operations Frameworks
