      WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS?

      • If your application is selected, you will be contacted by Joe to complete an online technical test, followed by a 30-minute HR pre-qualification
      • Next, you will have a technical interview via video call, covering SQL, Python, statistics, probability, analytics, and business sense
      • You will then have one week to complete an analytical Use Case and an AssessFirst test (personality, motivations, and reasoning)
      • After that, you will visit us in our offices to present your Use Case and meet Xavier – Senior Data Analytics Manager (your future manager), Iouri – Chief Data Officer, and a business stakeholder
      • During your visit to Bordeaux, you will also have a final HR interview, including a debrief on your AssessFirst test

      To ensure an optimal candidate experience, the Betclic recruitment process typically takes 4 to 6 weeks.
