ENTER THE GAME 🎮

As a Senior Product Designer, you will join Betclic’s Product Design team, a group of passionate designers working closely with Product Managers and Engineers to continuously improve the user experience across our Sports products.

This is a hands-on, mobile-first role, focused on solving complex UX problems in a high-traffic, real-time consumer environment.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

As a Senior Product Designer, you will work within one of our Sports product squads, depending on your background, strengths, and interests.

🏟️ Two possible squads within the Sports domain

1️⃣ Sport squad

This squad focuses on the core sports experience:

  • sports navigation and discovery

  • betting journeys and offers

  • match pages and live experiences

  • complex user flows, edge cases, and real-time constraints

  • using product data and insights to understand user behaviours, identify friction points, and inform design decisions

2️⃣ Gamification squad

This squad focuses on engagement and retention mechanics:

  • gamification features

  • challenges, missions, and rewards

  • progression systems and engagement loops

  • playful experiences that make sports more interactive and engaging

👉 The squad assignment will be discussed together, based on your profile and preferences.

WHAT YOU WILL DO 🚀

In both squads, you will:

  • Design new features based on user research, analytics, and business objectives

  • Simplify and optimise complex user journeys while ensuring clarity and consistency

  • Take end-to-end ownership of the user experience on your scope

  • Collaborate very closely with Product Managers and Engineers within a product squad

  • Lead or contribute to UX workshops (discovery, ideation, testing)

  • Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes using Figma

  • Contribute to and help evolve our design system

  • Stay up to date with UX/UI best practices, tools, and trends

WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR? 🔍

This role is for you if:

  • You have at least 8 years of experience as a Product Designer / Senior Product Designer

  • You have strong experience designing mobile-first B2C products

  • You are hands-on in Figma and enjoy staying close to the product

  • You are comfortable working on complex UX challenges (logic, flows, edge cases)

  • You are used to collaborating closely with PMs and Engineers in mature product teams

  • You have a strong interest in sports (you don’t need to be a betting expert, but curiosity and affinity matter)

  • You have strong communication skills, a team spirit, and a high attention to detail

  • You speak French and English fluently

NICE TO HAVES 🎯

Depending on the squad, it’s a plus if you have experience with:

  • Gamification, engagement, or retention mechanics

  • Sports, gaming, or mobile gaming products

  • Regulated consumer products

  • Design systems and scalable UI patterns

  • Data-informed design (analytics, experiments, funnels)


WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS? 📝

If your application is shortlisted, our recruiter will contact you within a week for an initial HR screening (30 minutes). Then, you will complete the AssessFirst test (personality, motivation and cognitive reasoning).

About two weeks later, you will either take a technical test with one of our design experts or work on a case study to present to the team.

Next, you will meet your future manager, followed by a member of the Executive or Management Committee.

Finally, the recruiter will conduct a final HR interview to review your AssessFirst results together.

The recruitment process usually takes between 4 to 6 weeks to ensure an optimal experience for you.
