Kickstart your data career with a rotation that takes you further — Data Analytics, Science, and Intelligence in one unique graduate journey.

ENTER THE GAME

We’re launching an innovative Graduate Data Program designed for ambitious minds eager to shape the future of data. Over the course of your journey, you’ll rotate across Data Analytics, Data Science, and Data Intelligence, gaining hands-on experience, mentorship, and exposure to real-world challenges that impact our business and our players. With a hybrid setup tailored for flexibility, you’ll sharpen your technical skills, broaden your perspective, and discover where your strengths shine best. This program is your fast track to becoming a well-rounded data professional, ready to drive insights, innovation, and impact.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC

Your mission will be :

  • Explore and manipulate large datasets to facilitate their use and analysis
  • Transform raw data into visualization tools that support decision-making
  • Work closely with business partners to understand their challenges and provide actionable recommendations
  • Explore our sports data to uncover insights for our machine learning models
  • Implement predictive models tailored to our business needs, optimizing the accuracy of both short- and long-term predictions
  • Identify high value-added opportunities to support data driven decision making

WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS?

  • If your application is selected, you will be contacted by Joe to complete an online technical test & AssessFirst, followed by a 15-minute HR pre-qualification
  • You’ll then meet your future managers for a fit and technical interview
  • After that, for the final round you will meet the Chief Data Officer, and you will also have a final HR interview, including a debrief on your AssessFirst test

In order to provide an optimal applicant experience, the overall hiring process at Betclic lasts 4 to 6 weeks.

WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR?

We’re looking for teammates who bring humor, respect, and kindness to the table—plus a bit of drive and a genuine passion for what they do!

This internship is for you if:

  • You’re in your final year of a Master’s degree (or equivalent, Bac +4/5) specializing in Data or Statistics
  • You have strong analytical skills, with rigor and the ability to synthesize information effectively
  • You’re eager to join a fast-paced and challenging professional environment
  • You have a solid foundation in SQL and want to sharpen your skills further
  • You have already built reports using a data visualization tool
  • You have good knowledge of a programming language (R or Python)
  • You’re motivated to grow your communication and presentation abilities
  • And finally, you’re fluent in English
