ENTER THE GAME

You will join the Data Analytics team, part of a larger Data team which also includes Data Science, Data Product Management and Product Analytics teams. The Analytics team mission is to grow the business by identifying critical insights and high value opportunities. You'll integrate a fast growing team of +30 passionate individuals tackling challenges of all sorts: from scaling marketing campaign analytics to building complex customer segmentations to predicting the outcome of the next football game.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC

As a Staff Data Analyst, you will serve as the strategic analytics partner for two of Betclic's most critical Product domains: Payment and Fraud/AML. Working closely with Product Directors and senior business stakeholders, you will ensure that every product initiative is guided by data, delivers measurable business impact, and contributes to Betclic's long-term strategic objectives.

Payment team mission is to deliver the most seamless, secure, and trusted payment experience for our players while optimizing payment costs and maintaining full compliance with regulatory requirements.

Fraud team mission is to continuously enhance the accuracy and coverage of our fraud detection and anti-money laundering models, with the long-term objective of achieving fully automated decision-making by 2030.

Your mission will be :

  • Partner with Product Directors and senior business stakeholders to develop a deep understanding of strategic priorities, business challenges, and future opportunities.
  • Deliver high-impact analyses and transform complex data into actionable insights that drive product improvements and measurable business outcomes.
  • Identify high-value opportunities to accelerate business growth and influence the evolution of product roadmaps
  • Lead complex analytical initiatives end-to-end, from problem framing and hypothesis definition through implementation and impact measurement.
  • Promote analytical excellence by ensuring that insights are robust, actionable, and aligned with business objectives.

As part of you Staff Data Analyst scope, you will be in charge of mentoring a team of three Data Analysts, fostering their professional development while maintaining a high standard of analytical quality across the team. You will also serve as the primary analytics partner for the Payments and Fraud & AML domains, collaborating closely with Product Managers, Product Directors, Engineering leaders, and senior executives to align priorities, shape product strategy, and maximize business value. This role offers significant exposure to executive leadership and requires the ability to communicate complex analytical findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Some examples of recent projects:

  • Fraud: Evaluated the performance of Genuine Score, Betclic's ML model designed to identify false-positive fraud detections, and identified opportunities to improve precision and operational efficiency
  • AML: Enhanced our in-house GenAI-powered AML detection engine by optimizing detection performance and strengthening safeguards against AI hallucinations
  • Payment: Analyzed abusive payment behaviors among customers in Côte d'Ivoire and designed strategies to mitigate fraudulent activity while preserving a frictionless experience for legitimate players

HIRING PROCESS

If your application is selected, you will be contacted by Joe to complete an online technical test, followed by a 30-minute HR pre-qualification

Next, you will have a technical interview via video call, covering SQL, Python, statistics, probability, analytics, and business sense

You will then have one week to complete an analytical Use Case and an AssessFirst test (personality, motivations, and reasoning)

After that, you will visit us in our offices to present your Use Case and meet Xavier - Senior Data Analytics Manager (your future manager), Iouri – Chief Data Officer, and a business stakeholder

During your visit to Bordeaux, you will also have a final HR interview, including a debrief on your AssessFirst test

In order to provide an optimal applicant experience, the overall hiring process at Betclic lasts 4 to 6 weeks.
