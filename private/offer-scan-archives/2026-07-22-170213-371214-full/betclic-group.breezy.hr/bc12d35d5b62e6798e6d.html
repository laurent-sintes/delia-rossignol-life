ENTER THE GAME 🎮

Following the combination of Betclic, Tipico, and Admiral, we are undertaking a major Finance Transformation to create a Combined Group Finance function within Banijay Gaming.

As Finance Transformation PMO, you will play a pivotal role in coordinating this strategic transformation program, ensuring robust governance, effective execution, and transparent reporting across multiple workstreams. Reporting to the VP Strategic Finance, you will work with senior stakeholders across Finance, Technology, HR, Legal, and other business functions to help build a best-in-class Finance organization supporting a €3bn revenue business operating across multiple jurisdictions.


YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

In this role, your main missions will be:

    Program Governance & Coordination

    • Coordinate the overall Finance Transformation roadmap and delivery plan.
    • Maintain the integrated program plan across all workstreams.
    • Ensure milestones, deliverables, dependencies, and critical paths are clearly identified and tracked.
    • Organize and facilitate governance forums, steering committees, and program reviews.
    • Prepare agendas, materials, minutes, and action trackers.

    Reporting & Performance Tracking

    • Develop and maintain program dashboards and reporting packs.
    • Track progress against objectives, milestones, timelines, and budget.
    • Monitor KPIs and transformation success metrics.
    • Produce executive-level reporting for senior leadership and steering committees

    Risk, Issue & Dependency Management

    • Maintain the program RAID log (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies).
    • Proactively identify risks and escalation needs.
    • Coordinate mitigation plans with workstream leaders.
    • Ensure critical decisions are documented and tracked.

    Stakeholder Management

    • Coordinate interactions between Finance, Technology, HR, Legal, and business stakeholders
    • Support communication activities across the transformation
    • Facilitate collaboration between teams located across multiple countries
