ENTER THE GAME 🎮

As a Product Manager in the Fraud Product Team, your role is to elevate our fraud and anti-money laundering detection engine to the next level.

YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

In this role, your main missions will include:

1. Deeply understand our users and our market

  • Grasp the challenges of fraud prevention

  • Conduct competitive and tech watch, and contribute to team-wide discussions

2. Own the Product Discovery phase

  • Identify impactful projects to combat fraud across the full customer lifecycle—from registration to closure

  • Manage relationships with key partners such as banks and payment providers to identify new opportunities

3. Contribute to the product strategy and roadmap

  • Help define the roadmap within your scope, frame projects, ensure timely delivery, measure outcomes, and iterate

  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders (countries, domains, IT, Customer Service, Compliance) to gather needs and share project updates

4. Build the best solution and measure its impact

  • Continuously improve our rule sets and algorithms

  • Monitor key performance indicators daily

  • Conduct functional analyses in case of incidents

  • Use SQL to analyze and manipulate data

WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR? 🔍

We are looking for passionate and curious professionals who bring kindness and a touch of enthusiasm!

This job is for you if:

  • You are passionate about both project management and fraud prevention, and excited by innovation and product design

  • You hold a Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Finance, or Data, with at least 5 years of experience in IT project management, ideally product management

  • You are autonomous and creative, capable of managing your projects end-to-end and collaborating cross-functionally

  • You are methodical, with a strong ability to take a step back and anticipate future needs

  • You are data-driven, fluent in SQL, and know how to make numbers speak and measure your impact

  • Knowledge of payments, fraud prevention, or cybersecurity is a plus

  • And finally, you speak English fluently

WHAT ARE THE RECRUITMENT STEPS? 📝

  • If your application is shortlisted, Benoit will contact you within a week for an initial HR screening (30 minutes). Then, you will complete the AssessFirst test (personality, motivation and cognitive reasoning).
  • You will meet Julien, Lead Product Manager, your future manager, followed by Simon, Director of Fraud and Catherine, Chief Payment Officer.
  • Finally, Benoit will conduct a final HR interview to review your AssessFirst results together.

The recruitment process usually takes around 4 weeks to ensure an optimal experience for you.
