Why join Caudalie?

Since Caudalie was established in 1995, it is thanks to our talented, passionate employees that we have succeeded in growing without ever having to compromise on our values and vision: that of skincare that is ever more effective, natural, clean and environmentally-friendly. We make a personal commitment to this.

At Caudalie, we also know that we foster creativity and innovation by giving our teams independence. So if you also enjoy entrepreneurial adventures, agility, reactivity, risk-taking... Join us, and let us build the Caudalie of tomorrow together!

Mathilde and Bertrand, Founders of Caudalie
