ENTER THE GAME 🎮

As GenAI Finance Ops Expert, you will play a key role in accelerating the adoption of Generative AI across Betclic's Finance organization. Working at the crossroads of Finance, Technology, Data, and Change Management, you will design and deliver AI-powered solutions that transform the way Finance teams operate.

You will identify high-impact opportunities, redesign Finance processes, deploy AI assistants and intelligent workflows, and drive the adoption of innovative ways of working. By partnering with Finance leaders, Product, Data, and Engineering teams, you will contribute to building a more efficient, scalable, and AI-enabled Finance organization.


YOUR ROLE WITHIN BETCLIC 🔥

In this role, your main missions will be:

    AI Strategy & Transformation

    • Define the future-state Finance operating model by redesigning Finance functions (FP&A, Treasury, Accounting, P2P, Consolidation, etc.) to fully leverage Generative AI capabilities.
    • Accelerate AI adoption across the Group Finance organization and establish a culture of AI-driven continuous improvement
    • Identify, evaluate, and prioritize GenAI opportunities across all corporate finance processes
    • Drive the Finance AI roadmap aligned with Finance leadership priorities

    Finance Process Optimization

    • Lead the delivery of GenAI-powered Finance applications, acting as product owner and project manager and leveraging from modern AI development tools
    • Support Finance teams in moving from manual activities toward augmented and autonomous processes.
    • Shadow and review existing processes within all finance team and identify efficiency opportunities
    • Implement workflow automation solutions leveraging GenAI technologies
    • Partner with Data, Product, Engineering, and Business teams to deliver solutions

    Enablement & Change Management

    • Own the GenAI Finance knowledge management framework, ensuring AI assets, prompts, documentation, standards, and best practices are maintained, shared, and continuously improved across the Finance organization.
    • Lead AI adoption initiatives across Finance teams
    • Design and deliver training programs, workshops, and coaching sessions
    • Promote best practices in prompting, AI-assisted analysis, and productivity
    • Coordinate enablement and change management strategy with the support of the GenAI Program central team
    • Track adoption, team onboarding and value creation KPIs.
